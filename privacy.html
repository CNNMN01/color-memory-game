Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

1. Introduction

At Color Memory Game ("we," "our," "us"), we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when using https://colourmemorygame.netlify.app.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Game Data: Scores and progress are stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Account Required: We do not ask for personal information or require registration to play.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Cookies: Used by Google AdSense for displaying advertisements.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-identifiable data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent playing to improve the Game.
  • Device Information: Basic details about the device (e.g., operating system, browser settings) may be collected for optimization.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Game.
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.
  • Understand player interaction to enhance user experience.
  • Detect and prevent technical issues.
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements.

4. Third-Party Services

We display ads through Google AdSense, which may use cookies or similar technologies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history.

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your Google Ads Settings.

For details, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All game data (scores, settings) remains stored locally in your browser. We do not transmit or store this data on external servers.
  • No User Accounts: We do not maintain personal accounts or profiles.
  • Security Measures: We implement reasonable safeguards to prevent unauthorized access or alterations to local data.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about the data collected.
  • Deletion: Clear your browser's local storage to delete game data.
  • Opt-Out: Disable personalized ads through Google settings.
  • Do Not Track: Enable your browser's Do Not Track feature.

7. Children's Privacy

The Game is suitable for all ages but is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

If you access the Game from outside the United States, your data may be processed in the U.S., where data-protection laws may differ from your region.

9. Legal Rights for Specific Regions

California (CCPA)

California residents may request access, deletion, or opt out of the sale of personal information without discrimination.

European Union (GDPR)

EU residents have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and object to processing, as well as data portability.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
