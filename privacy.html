Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Color Memory Game ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website at colourmemorygame.netlify.app.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

  • Game Data: Your game scores and progress are stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to play.

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Cookies: We use cookies for advertising purposes through Google AdSense.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you access and use our game, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent playing.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our game.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our game
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Understand how users interact with our game
  • Detect and prevent technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

What Google Collects:

  • Cookie IDs and similar technologies
  • IP address
  • Browser type and settings
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All game data (scores, settings) is stored locally in your browser. We do not transmit or store your game data on our servers.
  • No User Accounts: We do not require registration or maintain user accounts.
  • Security Measures: We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request information about the data we collect
  • Deletion: Clear your browser's local storage to delete game data
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising through Google's settings
  • Do Not Track: You can enable Do Not Track in your browser settings

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages but is not specifically directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Users

If you are accessing our game from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website or GitHub repository.
